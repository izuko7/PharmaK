About KEVOREH PHARMA

Based in the heart of Abidjan, Kevoreh Pharma is a cornerstone of health in Ivory Coast. We are committed to providing high-quality healthcare products that meet international standards of safety and efficacy.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between global medical innovation and local patient care through professional excellence and unwavering integrity. We believe that health is a fundamental right, and our infrastructure is designed to serve this belief.
